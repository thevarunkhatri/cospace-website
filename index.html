Working Together at Home

With WFH and co-working on the rise, we provide a platform for users to make their home into a co-working space to work with together with others.

What is CoSpace?

A mobile application that connects users looking for a co-working environment with a comfortable and shared spaces made by other users in their homes.

Two Types of Users

Host

  • Earn Passive Income
  • Low Upfront Costs
  • Social Interaction

Guest

  • Access to creative workspaces
  • Social Interaction
  • Affordable Pricing
